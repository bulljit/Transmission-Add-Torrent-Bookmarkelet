'Download Torrent' Bookmarklet Setup

These step-by-step instructions show you how to add a bookmark that will download the Torrents on the current page to your remote Transmission client.

Step 1: Bookmark This Page

Tap the 'Send' button in the toolbar at the bottom of the screen, then select Add Bookmark.

Take note of the name and bookmarks folder and then tap Save.

Step 2: Edit The Bookmark

Tap the Bookmarks button in the toolbar at the bottom of the screen.

Navigate through your bookmarks until you locate Download Torrent Remotely! in the list. Tap Edit to modify the list and then tap on the bookmark.

After the editing panel slides into view, tap in the second field (it begins with "http:"). You want to edit the beginning of this text, so tap and drag the loupe all the way to the left.

Place the editing point between the "?" and the "j" then tap the delete key until the editing point is at the beginning of the line. Then scroll right and find the 'myserver.ip:port' and replace this with your Transmission client server address and port number (seperating each with a colon). When you are done, your bookmark should look like this:

Step 3: Use The Bookmark

The next time you are on a page with Torrent(s) you want to download, open your bookmarks as before:

Then tap on Download Torrent Remotely!. The page will then be modified to include 'external link' icons next to all the Torrent links. Then click the link to download the Torrent you want and a new window will open which will send the Torrent file to your Transmission client. Once done, click the X to close the window, and repeat for any other Torrent you want from the page. That's it!
